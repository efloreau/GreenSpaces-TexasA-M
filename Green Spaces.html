Home

A collaborative project to develop a comprehensive webpage highlighting the lush green spaces scattered throughout the Texas A&M University campus. The webpage aims to showcase the campus's natural beauty, offering interactive maps and detailed descriptions of each green area, including parks, gardens, and recreational spots. Through photography and informative content, visitors will be able to explore the diverse landscapes and learn about the ecological significance of these green spaces. This initiative seeks to promote environmental awareness and appreciation while serving as a valuable resource for students, faculty, and visitors.
